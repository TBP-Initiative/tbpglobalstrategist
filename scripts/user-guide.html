1. Platform Overview

The TBP Global Strategist platform is your central workspace for managing your journey through the TBP Strategist Pathway. Key areas include:

  • Individual Dashboard — Your personalised home page with stats, pathway information, projects, activity feed, and submission tools
  • Profile — Your professional profile visible to other strategists and leadership
  • Projects — Browse and contribute to TBP projects
  • Strategists — Explore profiles of other strategists in the network
  • Admin Panel — (Admin users only) Manage users, stages, work areas, and system settings

2. The Individual Dashboard

2.1 Welcome & Stage Header

  • Your name and a personalised greeting
  • Your current stage badge
  • Stage description explaining what your current stage means
  • Assigned work areas shown as colour-coded pills
  • Visual stage progression indicator with your current position highlighted

2.2 Stats Cards

  • Active Projects — Number of projects you are contributing to
  • Collaborations — Active collaboration count and pending requests
  • Network Size — Your professional network on the platform
  • Publications — Your published work count

2.3 Progression Pathway

Shows your current stage details including focus, action tasks, typical outputs, and requirements for advancing to the next stage.

2.4 Submit Report / Task Feedback

Lets you upload reports and view your submission history for the current stage.

2.5 My Projects

  • List of your projects with status badges (Active, Draft, Completed, On Hold)
  • Progress bars showing completion percentage and deadline information
  • Click View all to see your complete project list

2.6 Recent Activity

Timeline of recent platform activities including project creations, collaboration requests, messages, and profile updates.

2.7 Right Sidebar

  • Notifications Panel — Unread notifications with mark-as-read functionality
  • Collaboration Requests — Incoming collaboration requests with accept/decline options
  • Saved Initiatives — Bookmarked initiatives for quick access
  • Quick Actions — Shortcut buttons for common tasks

3. Progression Pathway

3.1 What You Will See

  • Stage Title & Summary — Clear description of your current stage
  • Focus Area — What you should concentrate on at this stage
  • Actions & Tasks — A checklist of specific tasks to complete
  • Typical Outputs — Examples of the work products expected at your stage
  • Progress to Next Stage — A highlighted box showing requirements to move to the next stage

3.2 Stage Progression

Progressing through stages is managed by TBP administrators. When you have completed the required tasks and demonstrated the necessary capabilities, an administrator may promote you to the next stage. You will receive a notification when your stage changes.

Note: Stage progression is not automatic. It is based on demonstrated capability, task completion, and administrator review.

4. Managing Your Profile

4.1 Accessing Your Profile

  1. Click on your name or avatar in the navigation bar
  2. Select Profile from the dropdown menu
  3. Alternatively, navigate to Dashboard → Profile

4.2 Editing Your Profile

  1. Click the Edit Profile button on your profile page
  2. Update fields: Title, Bio (up to 2000 characters), Years of Experience, Availability, Category, Location, LinkedIn URL, Website URL
  3. Click Save to apply your changes
Tip: A complete profile increases your visibility and helps administrators assign you to suitable projects and work areas.

Note: The Hourly Rate field was previously part of the profile but has been removed as of Version 1.1.

4.3 Public Strategist Profile

Your public profile page includes your name, title, bio, stage badge, assigned work areas, current TBP Projects, and Strategist Focus card.

5. Browsing & Managing Projects

5.1 Browse Projects

  1. Navigate to Dashboard → Browse Projects
  2. Browse the list of available TBP projects
  3. Use the search bar to find specific projects
  4. Click on a project to view its details

5.2 Project Details

Each project page includes project title, description, category, tags, status indicator, milestones with progress tracking, media attachments, contributor list, and related projects.

Note: Access to certain project features may be restricted based on your current progression stage.

6. Submitting Reports & Task Feedback

6.1 How to Submit

  1. On your dashboard, locate the Submit Report / Task Feedback section
  2. Enter a Title for your submission
  3. Optionally add a Description with additional notes
  4. Upload your file using the drag-and-drop area (drag a file or click browse)
  5. Click Submit Report to upload

6.2 Supported File Formats

FormatExtensionUse Case
PDF.pdfReports, reflections, formal documents
Word.docx / .docWritten reports, memos, briefs
PowerPoint.pptx / .pptPresentations, pitch decks
Excel.xlsx / .xlsData analysis, spreadsheets, models
CSV.csvData sets, export files
Text.txtNotes, plain text documents
Rich Text.rtfFormatted text documents
OpenDocument Text.odtWord processing (LibreOffice)
OpenDocument Spreadsheet.odsSpreadsheets (LibreOffice)

6.3 Viewing Past Submissions

Your previous submissions are listed below the upload form with title, description, file type, size, submission date, and a download button.

Tip: Submissions are grouped by your current stage. When you advance, past submissions remain accessible.

7. Notifications

7.1 Types of Notifications

TypeExamples
Stage ChangesYou have been promoted to a new stage
Work Area AssignmentsYou have been assigned to new work areas
Project UpdatesA project you follow has been updated
Collaboration RequestsAnother strategist wants to collaborate
System AnnouncementsPlatform updates, new features, deadlines

7.2 Accessing Notifications

  • Bell Icon — Click the bell icon in the navigation bar
  • Notifications Panel — Your dashboard has a dedicated notifications panel in the right sidebar

7.3 Managing Notifications

  • Click the checkmark to mark a notification as read
  • Click the X to dismiss a notification
  • Unread notifications show with a blue dot indicator

8. Browsing Strategists

8.1 Accessing the Directory

  • Navigate to /strategists from the main navigation
  • Browse the grid of strategist cards showing name, title, stage, and location
  • Click on a strategist to view their full profile

8.2 Strategist Profile Page

Each strategist profile includes hero section, professional bio, current TBP Projects, Strategist Focus card, assigned work areas, featured project highlights, and recent activity feed.

9. Account Settings

9.1 Changing Your Password

  1. Navigate to Dashboard → Settings
  2. Click the Change Password section
  3. Enter your current password, new password, and confirm
  4. Click Save to update
Important: Choose a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

9.2 Notification Preferences

Manage which types of notifications you receive via Settings. You can enable or disable notifications for stage changes, work area assignments, project updates, collaboration requests, and system announcements.

9.3 Account Information

Your account email and basic information can be viewed in Settings. Contact an administrator to change your email address.

10. Troubleshooting & FAQ

10.1 I cannot log in

Check your email and password. Use Forgot Password to reset. Contact your administrator if the issue persists.

10.2 My dashboard shows the wrong stage

Your stage is managed by TBP administrators. Contact them if you believe your stage is incorrect.

10.3 I cannot see my work areas

Work areas are assigned by administrators. Check the Assigned areas section on your dashboard. Contact your administrator if needed.

10.4 My file upload failed

Ensure your file is in a supported format (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, CSV, TXT, RTF, ODT, ODS). Check file size limits. Refresh and try again.

10.5 I am not receiving notifications

Check your notification preferences in Settings. Ensure your browser allows notifications. Refresh the page.

10.6 How do I contact support?

Platform support is handled through your TBP organisation administrators. Contact them for account issues, stage progression, or platform questions.
